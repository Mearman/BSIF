## 9. Conclusion

**The formal methods field is experiencing significant growth and maturation:**

1. **Strong industrial adoption**: MongoDB (TLA+), Microsoft (Pact), Amazon (formal verification)
2. **AI integration**: AI-assisted specification becoming mainstream
3. **Accessibility**: Tools becoming easier through AI assistance
4. **Cloud-native**: Scalable verification services emerging
5. **Standards activity**: Multiple working groups forming
6. **Commercial activity**: Significant funding and acquisitions

**The trend is moving from academic research toward industrial implementation**, with particular focus on making formal methods more accessible and practical for real-world applications.
